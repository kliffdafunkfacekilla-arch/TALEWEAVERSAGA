## The Defection
The librarian of [[Magistar Lophex]]’s vault. She realized that the "knowledge" Lophex was gathering was actually a blueprint for universal annihilation. Sora memorized the counter-sequences for the Great Ritual, set fire to the physical archives, and escaped into the mountains with the only copy of the cure in her head.
