## The Defection
Sylas was [[Magistar Aurgenas]]’s master of spies. He was the one who discovered the Magistars' secret plot to sacrifice [[The Fae]]. He spent decades operating as a double agent, smuggling Fae families out of the Dominion through "Flux-rifts" before finally burning Aurgenas's intelligence network to the ground and fleeing to the gods.
