## The Defection
A high-architect for [[Magistar Metrion]]. Metrion ordered Thrum to design a city that would double as a massive execution chamber. Thrum followed the orders but included a "Logic-flaw" in the design. When Metrion tried to activate the trap, the entire structure collapsed on the Magistar's own legions instead.
