# Trade-Master Hrothe ("The Carrion King")

> **"I am not a predator, Ambassador. I am a janitor. I buy what you have broken."**

## 1.0 Command Profile
Captain of **[[The Consummate Condor]]**, the Empire's massive trade barge. Hrothe is the master of economic warfare.

* **Species:** Bearded Vulture.
* **Visuals:** A bald, scarred head with a ruffle of rust-colored feathers. He wears spectacles made of ground ruby (to filter the harsh sunlight of the upper atmosphere) and is constantly checking a pocket watch.

## 2.0 Philosophy: Carrion Economics
Hrothe applies the biology of a vulture to the **[[The Dragonstone Economy]]**.
* **The Tactic:** He does not aggressively hunt for deals; he circles failing economies. He waits for a city to starve, a mine to collapse, or a war to bankrupt a noble house.
* **The Swoop:** When the target is desperate, he lands the *Condor* and buys their assets for pennies on the dollar. He is hated by the **[[River Folk]]** (who prefer active haggling) but respected by the **[[Golden Warren]]** (who respect the math).
