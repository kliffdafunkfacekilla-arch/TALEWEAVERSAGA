### Dragonstone and Aether-Tech

The remnants of the shattered cosmic clockwork, crystalized fragments imbued with divine energy, now serve as the technological foundation of the modern world. These fragments, known as **Dragonstone**, function as batteries, holding immense reserves of the raw chaos energy that bleeds from the dragon prisons.

The key to harnessing this power lies in creating conduits that channel the stone's volatile power. This fusion of material and engineering is known as **Aether-Tech**. These devices use precisely tuned metal coils and specialized conductors of various different metals, gemstones and other materials to convert the chaotic energy  into the desired and usable force, such as kinetic propulsion for airships, industrial heat, or electricity.

However, this technology is profoundly dangerous. A failure in an Aether-tech device results in a catastrophic containment breach known as a **"Blowout."** This is no mere explosion; it is a leak of raw chaos into the material world, causing localized reality-warping events such as gravity reversals, the instantaneous petrification of living tissue, and other physics-defying phenomena.
